Job Title: Data Scientist

Company Overview:
We are a technology-driven team that leverages data to inform strategy across product, marketing, and operations.

Role Overview:
We are seeking a Data Scientist to analyze large datasets, develop predictive models, and deliver insights that influence business direction.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze structured and unstructured data to extract actionable insights
- Build and validate machine learning models for classification and regression
- Conduct feature engineering and data preprocessing
- Create dashboards and reports to communicate findings
- Partner with cross-functional teams to deploy models

Required Qualifications:
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Experience with machine learning libraries such as scikit-learn, PyTorch, or TensorFlow
- Solid understanding of statistics and probability
- Experience with data visualization tools

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Familiarity with NLP or deep learning techniques
