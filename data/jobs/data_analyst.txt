Job Title: Data Analyst

Company Overview:
We are a data-centric organization committed to informed decision-making across all business units.

Role Overview:
We seek a Data Analyst to analyze complex datasets, create data visualizations, and support business stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and transform raw data
- Produce analytical reports and dashboards
- Perform exploratory data analysis
- Communicate results to non-technical stakeholders
- Identify patterns and anomalies in datasets

Required Qualifications:
- Proficiency in SQL for data extraction
- Strong skills in Excel or similar tools
- Experience with data visualization (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- Basic understanding of statistical analysis

Preferred Qualifications:
- Familiarity with Python or R
- Experience handling unstructured data
