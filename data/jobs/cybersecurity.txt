Job Title: Cybersecurity Specialist

Company Overview:
We are dedicated to securing and protecting digital infrastructure and sensitive information.

Role Overview:
We are looking for a Cybersecurity Specialist to monitor systems, identify threats, and implement security solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
- Perform security risk assessments
- Monitor networks for suspicious activity
- Implement safeguards and incident response procedures
- Investigate and remediate vulnerabilities
- Document security policies and procedures

Required Qualifications:
- Strong understanding of cybersecurity frameworks
- Experience with security tools and SIEM solutions
- Knowledge of networking fundamentals
- Ability to analyze logs and security alerts

Preferred Qualifications:
- Certifications (CISSP, CEH, etc.)
- Experience in cloud security
