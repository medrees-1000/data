Job Title: Software Engineer

Company Overview:
We are a fast-paced engineering team building scalable and reliable software products used by millions of customers.

Role Overview:
We are looking for a Software Engineer to design, develop, test, and maintain full-stack applications.

Key Responsibilities:

- Collaborate with product and design teams

- Strong programming skills in Python, Java, or C++
- Experience with databases and REST APIs
- Familiarity with containerization tools (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Solid understanding of version control systems (e.g., Git)

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines
